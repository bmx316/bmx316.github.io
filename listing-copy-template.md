# Listing Copy Template

Copy/paste into MachineTools.com, Surplus Record, Machinio, eBay, etc.
Fill in the brackets. Keep the structure the same — buyers skim 50 listings a day, and consistent format wins.

---

## Whole machine listing

```
[YEAR] STUDER [MODEL] [Universal Cylindrical / ID / OD] Grinder

S/N: [serial number]
Year of manufacture: [YYYY]
Control: [Manual / Studer Granit / Fanuc 0i / Fanuc 31i / Siemens 840D]
Swing: [diameter] · Between centers: [length]
Max workpiece weight: [kg or lb]
Hours / cycles: [if known, else "unknown"]

CONDITION
Fully rebuilt in [year] by current owner — a machinist with [N] years
specializing in Studer grinders. Last produced production parts in
[month/year]. Powered, will be demonstrated under power on site.

INCLUDED
  - [list every wheel head, tailstock, steady rest, chuck, dresser]
  - [any in-process or check tooling]
  - Original Studer manuals (operating + electrical schematics)
  - [any spare parts going with the machine]

CONDITION OF KNOWN WEAR ITEMS
  - Way condition: [scraped in / production / worn but functional]
  - Hydraulics: [recently serviced / dry / unknown]
  - Wheel spindle: [recent service / runs true / needs check]

PRICE
Asking $[number] OBO. Buyer responsible for rigging and freight.
Trades considered.

LOCATION & LOADING
[City, State, USA].
Loading: [forklift available / dock-height / ground level / upper floor].

CONTACT
[Name]

Serious inquiries only. Wire transfer required, funds confirmed before
removal. As-is, where-is.
```

## Tips for great listings

1. **First photo is the whole machine, clean, well-lit, square-on.** That photo decides whether anyone clicks.
2. **Second photo: the nameplate.** Buyers want to verify model and year.
3. **Third photo: the control panel powered on.** Proves it works.
4. **Always include the serial number in the title text.** It comes up in dealer searches.
5. **List your phone number.** Dealers call; they don't fill out web forms.
6. **Don't write paragraphs.** Use the bulleted format above. Buyers scan, they don't read.
7. **Use both metric and imperial where it matters** (Studer is European, end-users are US — write "Swing: 200 mm (7.87 in)").
8. **Update the listing every 30 days.** Most platforms boost recently-edited listings in search.
