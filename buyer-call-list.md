## The email script (copy/paste)

```
Subject: Studer grinder shop — full liquidation — Big List attached

Hi,

I'm a machinist in [city, state] with a warehouse of Studer cylindrical
grinders (rebuilt by me over the last [N] years), CNC machines, plus
parts, tooling, and supplies. I am liquidating the entire inventory.

Attached is the full list — model, serial, year, condition, photos,
and a short video on the machines that power up.

I am soliciting:
  (a) outright purchase offers for the whole lot, or
  (b) outright offers on individual machines, or
  (c) auction / consignment proposals.

Please let me know which you'd like to do and your best number. Happy
to host a visit if you're seriously interested.

Thanks,
[Name]
[Phone]
[Email]
```

Send this to every contact in Tiers 1–3 in a single afternoon. Most will reply within a week.
